Fried Rice

Know I like Rice!

As we all know, this is and will always be my very favorite food

I've never used this recipe, but I will be trying it. Only put it for the sake of this project

Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • Ground pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 4 cups rice
  • soy sauce

Steps

  • beat eggs with pepper and salt
  • heat wok, add oil, add eggs, cook, clean pan
  • add more oil. Add rice. stir fry and add in soy sauce
  • stir in rest of ingredients and continue to cook
